Node.js Web Development (Full-Stack Project)

This Websit based on JavaScript project is my personal idea and design. The front-end is based on HTML with partial JQuery, Js and CSS. The back-end server is based on Node.js

1.Students can get first-hand information of what they want to know from people who already know that, such as how's school's dormitory or how's the feeling of being one of Fordham member or how's the food in our kitchen, some life questions and course questions can be posted and published.

2.Students can share their own resources with others who are registered in our website. The resources can be anything without any limitation (of course, in the legal range) such as course notes, course summary, some solutions based on question, some preparation guide for any kind of exams including FPA or some other difficult examinations.

3.Students can post renting information to find others to live together, which is a big problem when we first living outside from home.

4.Someone who graduated from school could share their experience of hunting job including how to pass the interview or what should prepare before interview.

5.Students can also come up with some great theme to discuss with people who are interested in.
